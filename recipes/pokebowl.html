Pokebowl

Description

A pokebowl can consist of many different things. Nevertheless, there are quite a few things that make up most pokebowls.

First, there should be a basis like rice, bulgur, couscous or quinoa.

Secondly, you can add a protein of your choice, for example Bacon, fish, Tofu or beans.

There is not really an end to a pokebowl, so go on and add fresh vegetables like carrots, cucumber and fruits like mango or grapefruit to add another note. You are really the own maker of your pokebowl.

The only thing keeping in mind is to keep the whole bowl not to dry so you might want to add a sauce or vegetable oil. And of course, don't overload your bowl so it spills whlie eating. Trust me. I speak (or rather write) from experience.

This is a recipe for a vegan pokebowl.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • smoked tofu
  • edamame
  • avocado
  • tomatoes
  • grapefuit
  • balsamico dressing

Steps

  1. boil the rice
  2. fry the tofu
  3. cut the avocado and the tomatoes
  4. peel and prepare the grapefruit
  5. mix everthing together in a bowl
  6. top it of with the balsamico dressing

Tip: Let the rice cool down, but enjoy the pokebowl while the rice is warm.
